Figma components are reusable design elements that help maintain consistency across your designs. They function as templates that can be duplicated and modified while maintaining a connection to the original master component.


Components are a cornerstone of design systems, allowing designers to create scalable and consistent user interfaces. When you update a master component, all instances of that component can be updated across your designs, ensuring design consistency and saving significant time during revisions.
